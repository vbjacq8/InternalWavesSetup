# List Of Materials
Below are a list of materials, with items in parenthesis referring to the specific implementation details in our experiment. Figures containing setup details not shown in the manuscript may be viewed in the [media folder](media).

## Creating Waves
* 2D Acrylic Wavetank and Platform (Engineering Laboratory Design, Inc. 18'-0" Sediment Flume; 203 long cm x 46 tall x 8 cm thick)
* 1 Saltwater Tank (A generic fish tank will do; 90 cm long x 42 cm wide x 40 cm tall) 
* 1 Freshwater Tank (A generic fish tank will do; 90 cm long x 30 cm wide x 58 cm tall)
* Various Aluminum Extrusion (80/20)
* 2 Peristaltic Pumps (BT-300EA JIHPUMP) with accompanying tubing; if mechanical pumps are used, mechanical pump tubing is required.
* Bilge Pump (Maxzone Bilge Pump 1100GPH Mod. A01-111001)
* Diffuser (DIY Sponge Diffuser bordered by foam walls)
* Computable Motors (Parker Automation Daedel Division for Topography Oscillation, Lintech Motor for Conductivity measurements)
* Smooth Topography (DIY styrofoam cut with hot-wire and stencil; max height of 3.14 cm, half-length of 8.58 cm)
* Salt for stratifying (Diamond Crystal Solar Naturals Salt Crystals)
* Power Distribution Unit
* Motor Controllers (Parker ZETA4 Drive Compumotor; 6K2 2-Axis Servo/Stepper Controller Compumotor)
  
## Measurement & Observation 
* Conductivity Rig (Thermo Scientific Orion Star A215 Conductivity Meter)
* Handheld Densitometer (Anton Paar DMA 35 Basic)
* 3 High-resolution, High-speed Cameras (Bobcat IGV-B3320)
* Parallel Ray Light Source (Heat Lamp)
* Screen (Foam whiteboard; 254 cm long x 121 cm tall x 2 cm thick)
* Projector (Optoma HD20)
* Pulse Generator for Camera Synchronization
